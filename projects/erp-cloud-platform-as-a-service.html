Description

The BIPIX Cloud Platform is a multitenant cloud ERP system. A flexible, easy to use and complete service to manage Business Processes and Communications all of them. The purpose of the system is to simplify and unify their Business Processes and Communications making their organizations more efficient, easier to manage and change and significantly reduce and accurately forecast their expenses. The system is targeted to facilitate growth of SME's worldwide. The aim is to do it by improving the technology and processes that currently SME's use. We have created the platform and modules to be as simple and as intuitive as possible.
No longer will trainings be need. No longer will companies depend on third parties to fully operate and make changes. The true success of BPX is the powerhouse central engine running at the heart of our platform making hard tasks looks easy and complex processes as simple as it can get. Flexibility makes the difference BPX can manage A to Z business needs: CRM, SRM, Project Management, Accounting, Human Resources, Inventory, Document management, Email and Web. It is all included. In addition, if necessary all these modules can work as standalone integrated with third parties.
