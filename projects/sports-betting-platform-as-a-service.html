Description

The sport betting platform known as “OddsMatrix v2” delivers a sleek and modern front end and integrates with your user system for seamless log in and out as well as a wallet solution for instantaneous transfers for your players. OddsMatrix v2 manages the sportsbook, sets odds on a huge variety of events, manages risk on the both event and player level, and settle bets. You can even use our betting license from Malta, EU. OddsMatrix v2 Sportsbook is a fully-managed, API based, flexible, modular and modern sportsbook platform offering an extensive range of features leading to business growth and punters satisfaction. The OddsMatrix Sportsbook platform has an architecture designed for horizontal scalability and high-availability. As a time-tested sport betting supplier, we envisioned a modern and modular sportsbook software solution that offers a wide range of features leading to business growth and punters satisfaction. Controlling the front-end user experience without having any restrictions imposed by the platform provider is an essential aspect for any ambitious sportsbook operator.
With the OddsMatrix v2 Sportsbook operators can go fully creative with a unique customizable website based on our sports APIs or use our proven template solutions for quick delivery. With the OddsMatrix Web APIs, operators can leverage the OddsMatrix Sportsbook platform for complex business logic while delivering entirely custom and unique user experience. In the box sports betting technology can be easily integrated with existing platforms, and operators uphold total autonomy over their betting sites and apps. OddsMatrix Data Feeds covers 35,000+ LIVE events per month and provides odds, stats, scores, settlements, and hosted match visualization widgets, all backed up by our performant trading tools. With 2,800+ esports events, the OddsMatrix Esports Services has the best coverage in the industry with the most engaging markets in esports today. Get everything you need from us. OddsMatrix Aggregator is a unique sports data aggregation technology which allows bookmakers to combine odds, settlements, and score feeds from multiple providers into a single aggregated feed.
