  • DATA SCIENCE

Description

Platform know as “Wealthy Kid” is a family communication platform. Enables safe communication with your children. Wealthy Kid is an application for kids to chat, share emotions and health data with parents taken via wearables. Mobile application allows to monitor mental state of your child, get detailed information, ask your kid and in case of need get recommendation from professionals.
With the Wealthy Kid every family has the opportunity to share emotions, health data, GPS location and communicate with the most important people in their life. Wealthy Kid helps to kid develop emotional self-consciousness while playing with favorite cartoon hero. Wealthy Kid connects children only with relatives, giving understanding what is going in mental in physical life of kid. Animated buttons will turn the app into best game ever.
